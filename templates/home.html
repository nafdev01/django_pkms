Welcome to PKMS!

PKMS is a powerful and easy-to-use knowledge management system designed to help students organize their notes, store important terms, and set revision goals. With PKMS, you can streamline the management of your academic study materials through an easy-to-use interface, making your learning experience more efficient and effective.

Features

Note Taking and Organization

Easily take and organize notes hierarchically by course, topic, and subtopic. This hierarchical structure allows for easy navigation and quick access to your notes.

Glossary

Store important terms and definitions for different courses in one central location, making it easier to study and review key concepts.

Revision Planning

Set revision goals and deadlines, and track your progress, helping you stay on top of your studies and achieve your academic goals.

Customizable Profile

Personalize your profile with a variety of settings, such as changing your profile picture or choosing a preferred color scheme.

Note Sharing

Share your notes with others via email, making it easy to collaborate and study with friends and classmates.

Privacy

All your notes and account information are kept secure and private, visible only to you.You also have the option to delete your account and completely exit the platform.
